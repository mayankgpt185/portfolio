Beyond Research: Interests & Hobbies

Sports Enthusiast

I have a deep passion for various sports, with fútbol being at the top of my list. I’m an avid supporter of Real Madrid, and my love for the game has grown through watching unforgettable matches and following legendary players. Aside from fútbol ⚽️, I also enjoy following Football 🏈, Basketball, Formula One, and Cricket. Each of these sports brings something unique to the table, whether it's the fast-paced nature of Formula One or the strategic depth of cricket.

Outdoor Activities

Being in nature is one of my greatest joys. I love hiking, camping and cycling. The serenity of the mountains or the rush of cycling through forest paths never gets old. I enjoy the challenge and the peace that comes from being outdoors, and I hope to explore even more trails and paths in the future.

Urdu Literature

Urdu literature has always held a special place in my heart. From the profound poetry of Allama Iqbal to the timeless prose of Faiz Ahmed Faiz, I find myself captivated by the beauty and depth of their words. I believe literature transcends barriers, and through Urdu poetry, I am constantly inspired by lines such as:

علم جب ہوگا، کِدھر جانا ہے، تب تک تو گزر جانا ہے۔
("When we become wise and know where to go, by then, we will have passed away")
These words resonate with me deeply, reflecting both the complexity and simplicity of human emotions.
